    Make a Difference with British Energy Helpline: Your Donations Matter


    Dear Supporters,

    Climate change and fuel poverty are critical issues facing our communities today. At British Energy Helpline, we are dedicated to addressing these challenges by promoting energy efficiency and supporting those in need. As an independent charitable trust, our mission is to reduce CO2 emissions, create energy-efficient homes, and ensure comfort for all occupants.

    How Your Donation Helps:


    • Supporting Low-Income Households: Your donations allow us to assist families who struggle with high energy bills or can't afford to upgrade their homes for better energy efficiency.
    • Providing Free Consultations: We offer free advice on government schemes, grants, and energy-saving measures to help households reduce their energy consumption and costs.
    • Raising Awareness: We educate the public about their rights, available government assistance, and practical energy-saving tips to combat climate change.
    • Advocating for Change: We work with local authorities, energy suppliers, Ofgem, and the UK government to advocate for those in fuel poverty and push for systemic changes.

    Why Your Support is Crucial:


    1. Combating Climate Change: Reducing energy consumption in homes and businesses is a vital step in lowering greenhouse gas emissions and tackling climate change.
    2. Alleviating Fuel Poverty: Many families are forced to choose between heating their homes and other essential needs. Your support helps us provide immediate and long-term solutions.
    3. Ensuring Energy Efficiency: Energy-efficient homes are not only better for the environment but also more comfortable and cost-effective for occupants.

    How to Donate:


    Making a donation to British Energy Helpline is simple and impactful. Your contributions will directly support our initiatives and help us expand our reach to more households in need.

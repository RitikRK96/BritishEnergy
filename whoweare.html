    Who we Are?

    British Energy Helpline is an independent charity dedicated to addressing fuel poverty and the climate emergency in the UK. We serve as a respected and trusted voice for the poor and vulnerable, tirelessly working to uplift those who struggle to afford high energy bills.

    Our Mission


    • Empowerment through Awareness: We run campaigns to educate the public about their rights, government schemes, energy-saving tips, and climate change.
    • Advocacy: We represent your issues to local authorities, energy suppliers, Ofgem, and the UK government.
    • Support for Energy Efficiency: We assist families who cannot afford to upgrade their energy efficiency measures, helping them comply with the latest regulations.
    • Access to Grants: We facilitate access to various government grants and schemes.
    • Free Consultations: We offer free advice on how to reduce energy bills.

    At British Energy Helpline, we are committed to supporting and empowering our community to achieve a more sustainable and affordable energy future.
